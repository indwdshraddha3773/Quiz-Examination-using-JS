Follow The Given Guideline


    Read and understand the guidelines for the test in advance and prepare for it accordingly

    Be aware of the exam format, such as fill-in, short answer, multiple-choice, or a combination of different types of questions.

    Attend an orientation session conducted by your institution for online exams or watch support videos

    Start solving the practice test and self-assess yourself, to increase efficiency.

    Work on time management and calculate how much time is required on each question; this exercise can help complete the exam within the stipulated time frame.

    Ensure that you have the correct schedule of examinations and login credentials for the same

    Ensure that you have submitted your final answers and have received confirmation of online submission.
